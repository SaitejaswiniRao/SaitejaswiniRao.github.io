Fabrication

Vinata is a sophisticated greeting robot, carefully designed and fabricated at Vignan's University. It is part of an ongoing initiative to blend cutting-edge robotics with daily university interactions, serving as a welcoming presence on campus.

Achievements

Vinata is a greeting robot fabricated and deployed in Vignan's University. Below are some of its key achievements and awards:

  • Awarded "Best Robotics Innovation 2023" at the National Tech Expo
  • Successfully interacted with over 5,000 visitors at Vignan’s University
  • Implemented advanced AI algorithms to enhance user engagement
  • Featured in TechTalks Magazine as one of the top university robotics projects
  • Reduced operational costs by 15% through energy-efficient design
